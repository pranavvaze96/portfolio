About

I'm a Product Manager who specialises in AI-native products. At MisticusMind, I own end-to-end product lifecycle for two B2B SaaS platforms — ContractGenius.ai and TheLegalGenius.ai — from ideation through iterative delivery. I work at the intersection of user needs, technical feasibility, and business outcomes, translating complex requirements into clear roadmaps that teams can actually ship against.
